About Me

Hi! I am Deepansh Sood. I was born in 1995 and spent most of my youth in Himachal Pradesh, India. I currently live in Bengaluru and write code for companies such as Wipro CRS and previously Ants Digital. Before that I studied Computer Science at Amity University.

Did I tell you the things I like? I have been fascinated by computers since my childhood and recently found myself involved with learning Rust and Haskell. I always enjoy writing Python. I am inspired by the Open Source community and would very much like to contribute.

Apart from that, I have broad a taste in music and video games. I also love skateboarding.
